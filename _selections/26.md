The names of nearly all the gods came to Greece from Egypt. I have ascertained through my research that they came from non-Greeks, and I think they came especially from Egypt. [2] Apart from Poseidon and the Dioscuri,<sup><a href="#note-1">1</a></sup> as I previously mentioned, and Hera, Hestia, Themis, the Graces, and the Nereids, the names of all the other gods have always existed in Egypt. I am merely repeating what the Egyptians themselves say. Regarding the names of the gods which they do not recognize, I think they were so named by the Pelasgians, except for Poseidon, whom the Greeks learned about from the Libyans. [3] No one except the Libyans has always known the name of this god and worshiped him.
