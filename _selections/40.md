Another spendthrift was Aethiops of Corinth, whom Archilochus mentions. Because of his love of pleasure and lack of self-control, when he was sailing to Sicily with Archias who was going to found Syracuse, he sold to his messmate the plot of land which he had obtained by lot in Syracuse for a honey-cake.
