It would be very foolish to say that the Ionians of the Twelve Cities are more Ionian or more noble than other Ionians, since not the smallest part of them are the Abantes from Euboea, who are not Ionians even by name, and Minyans from Orchomenos have been mixed in with them, along with Cadmeans and Dryopians and breakaway Phocians and Molossians and Pelasgians from Arcadia and Dorians from Epidaurus, along with many other ethnicities. [2] Even those of them who set out from the town hall at Athens and think they are the most noble of the Ionians did not bring women with them to the colony but took Carian wives, after murdering their parents.
