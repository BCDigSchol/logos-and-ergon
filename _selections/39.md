Mytilene produced famous men a long time ago, such as Pittacus, one of the Seven Wise Men, and the poet Alcaeus, as well as his brother Antimenidas, who Alcaeus says accomplished a great deed while fighting for the Babylonians and rescued them from danger by killing a warrior who stood only a palm’s width short of five royal cubits.<sup><a href="#note-1">1</a></sup>

<blockquote>
You have come from the ends of the earth, holding
the hilt of your sword made of ivory covered in gold.
</blockquote>
