preface: "The so-called Cup of Nestor was discovered in a grave on the island of Pithecusae, a Greek colony in Italy. The inscription scratched onto it, in epic-style language and verse, is one of the oldest known inscriptions in the Greek alphabet. It seems to be alluding to the story of a cup owned by the Homeric hero Nestor, described at Iliad 11.632-37."
