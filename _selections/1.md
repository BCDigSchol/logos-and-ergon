\tThe Phaeacians once dwelled in broad Hypereia,\n
5\tnear the oppressive Cyclopes,\n
\twho kept harassing them and were stronger in force.\n
\tThen god-like Nausithoos moved them\n
\tand he settled them in Scheria, far from hard-working men.\n
\tHe drove a wall around the city and built houses,\n
10\tand made temples of the gods and distributed the fields.
