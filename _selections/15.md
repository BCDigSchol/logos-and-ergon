Miletus had many achievements, the greatest of which is the number of colonies it founded. For they colonized the whole Black Sea and the Sea of Marmara and many other places. Anaximenes of Lampsacus says that Miletus settled the islands of Icarus and Lerus, and in the Hellespont region they settled Limnae in the Chersonnese, along with Abydos, Arisba, and Paeson on the Asian side of the Hellespont. They also founded Artace and Cyzicus on the island of Cyzicus, as well as Scepsis in the interior of the Troad.
